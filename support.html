Support

This app is designed to be simple, private, and fully offline. If you have questions or encounter issues, please read the information below.

How the App Works

The app allows you to confirm your status by responding once per day. After each response, you may optionally choose to send a message to selected contacts using your system’s messaging or email applications.

The app does not send any messages automatically and does not communicate with any servers.

Messaging and Notifications

All messages are sent manually by the user through system apps such as Messages or Mail. The app itself does not transmit, store, or monitor any outgoing communication.

All reminders and alerts are local notifications generated on your device only.

Data and Privacy

All data is stored locally on your device. No accounts are required, and no personal data is collected or transmitted. Deleting the app will permanently remove all stored data.

Important Notice

This app is not an emergency service, safety tool, or medical device. It does not automatically alert contacts or authorities under any circumstances.
